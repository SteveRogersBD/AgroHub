# Community Feature User Guide

## Overview
The Community tab is your social hub for connecting with other farmers, sharing experiences, and learning from the agricultural community.

## Features

### 1. Viewing the Community Feed

**How to Access:**
- Tap the "Community" icon in the bottom navigation bar
- The feed will automatically load posts from users you follow

**What You'll See:**
- Posts from farmers you follow
- User avatars and names
- Post content (text and images)
- Like and comment counts
- Timestamps showing when posts were created

**Feed States:**
- **Loading**: A spinner appears while posts are being fetched
- **Empty**: "No posts yet" message if you haven't followed anyone or they haven't posted
- **Error**: Error message with a "Retry" button if something goes wrong

### 2. Creating a Post

**How to Create:**
1. Tap the "+" button in the top-right corner of the Community screen
2. Enter your post content in the text field
3. (Optional) Tap "Add Image" to select a photo from your gallery
4. (Optional) Tap "Upload Image" to upload the selected photo
5. Tap "Post" in the top-right corner to publish

**Post Guidelines:**
- Maximum 5000 characters for text content
- At least some text is required
- Images are optional but enhance engagement
- You can remove a selected image before posting

**Post Creation States:**
- **Idle**: Ready to create a post
- **Uploading**: Image is being uploaded (progress indicator shown)
- **Creating**: Post is being created (progress bar shown)
- **Success**: Automatically returns to feed with your new post
- **Error**: Error message displayed, you can retry

### 3. Liking Posts

**How to Like:**
- Tap the heart icon below any post
- The heart fills in and turns green when liked
- The like count increases by 1

**How to Unlike:**
- Tap the filled heart icon again
- The heart becomes outlined
- The like count decreases by 1

**Features:**
- Instant feedback (optimistic updates)
- Your like status is saved across sessions
- See total like count for each post

### 4. Commenting on Posts

**How to View Comments:**
- Tap anywhere on a post card to expand it
- Comments section appears below the post
- Existing comments are loaded automatically

**How to Add a Comment:**
1. Expand a post by tapping on it
2. Scroll to the bottom of the comments section
3. Type your comment in the text field
4. Tap the send icon (paper plane) to post

**Comment Features:**
- See commenter's avatar and username
- View comment timestamps
- Comments are displayed in chronological order
- "No comments yet" message if post has no comments

**Comment Guidelines:**
- Maximum 2000 characters per comment
- Comments cannot be empty
- Be respectful and constructive

### 5. User Profiles in Posts

**User Information Displayed:**
- **Avatar**: Profile picture or initial letter if no picture
- **Username**: Displayed name of the poster
- **Timestamp**: When the post was created
  - "Just now" for posts less than 1 minute old
  - "Xm ago" for posts less than 1 hour old
  - "Xh ago" for posts less than 24 hours old
  - "Xd ago" for posts less than 7 days old
  - Full date for older posts

### 6. Post Images

**Image Display:**
- Images are displayed with proper aspect ratio
- Tap to view full size (future feature)
- Images are loaded efficiently with caching

**Image Upload:**
- Select from your device gallery
- Upload to media service
- Preview before posting
- Remove and reselect if needed

## Tips for Best Experience

### Engagement Tips
1. **Post Regularly**: Share your farming experiences, challenges, and successes
2. **Use Images**: Posts with images get more engagement
3. **Be Descriptive**: Provide context and details in your posts
4. **Engage with Others**: Like and comment on posts from other farmers
5. **Ask Questions**: The community is here to help

### Content Ideas
- Daily farm updates
- Crop progress photos
- Weather impact on your farm
- Pest or disease identification requests
- Success stories and harvests
- Equipment recommendations
- Farming tips and tricks
- Seasonal advice

### Privacy and Safety
- Only share information you're comfortable making public
- Don't share sensitive personal information
- Report inappropriate content (future feature)
- Block users if needed (future feature)

## Troubleshooting

### "Failed to load feed"
**Possible Causes:**
- No internet connection
- Backend services are down
- Authentication token expired

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Tap the "Retry" button
3. Log out and log back in
4. Contact support if issue persists

### "Failed to create post"
**Possible Causes:**
- No internet connection
- Post content is empty
- Image upload failed
- Server error

**Solutions:**
1. Ensure you have internet connection
2. Check that your post has content
3. Try uploading a smaller image
4. Retry the post creation

### "Failed to load comments"
**Possible Causes:**
- Network timeout
- Post was deleted
- Server error

**Solutions:**
1. Collapse and re-expand the post
2. Refresh the feed
3. Check your internet connection

### Images not loading
**Possible Causes:**
- Slow internet connection
- Image URL is invalid
- Media service is down

**Solutions:**
1. Wait for images to load
2. Refresh the feed
3. Check your internet connection
